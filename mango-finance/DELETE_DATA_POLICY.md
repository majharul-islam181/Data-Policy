# Mango Finance - Data Deletion Policy

**Last Updated:** March 18, 2026

---

## 🗑️ Delete Your Data

We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. You can delete all your personal financial information from **Mango Finance** at any time, directly from within the app.

---

## 📱 How to Delete Your Data

### **Quick Steps:**

1. **Open the Mango Finance app**
2. **Go to the Settings screen** (tap the Settings icon at the bottom)
3. **Navigate to the "Data" section** (under Settings)
4. **Tap the "Reset All Data" button** (red/delete button)
5. **Confirm the deletion** when the warning dialog appears
6. **Done!** All your data is permanently deleted

**⏱️ Time to complete:** Less than 1 minute

**⚡ When is data deleted:** Immediately (no waiting period)

**🔄 Can data be recovered:** No, deletion is permanent and irreversible

---

## 📊 What Data Gets Deleted

When you select "Reset All Data" in Mango Finance, **ALL of the following information is permanently deleted** from your device:

### **✅ Financial Transactions**
- Income records (salary, bonuses, gifts, etc.)
- Expense records (food, transport, shopping, etc.)
- Transfer records (between your accounts)
- All transaction details (amounts, dates, descriptions, notes)
- Transaction categories
- Transaction receipts and attachments

### **✅ Account Information**
- Bank account records
- Cash account details
- Credit card account information
- Wallet account records
- Account names and descriptions
- Account balances and history
- Account colors and icons
- Account types and settings

### **✅ Budget Data**
- Monthly budget limits
- Category-specific budgets
- Budget tracking progress
- Budget settings and preferences
- Spent amount records
- Remaining budget amounts

### **✅ Debt & Lending Records**
- Money you lent to others
- Money you borrowed from others
- Debtor/creditor information
- Debt settlement status
- Repayment history
- Debt amounts and due dates
- Contact names associated with debts
- Phone numbers associated with debts

### **✅ Reminders & Notifications**
- Reminder records
- Reminder titles and descriptions
- Reminder schedules
- Notification preferences
- Reminder activity history

### **✅ User Preferences & Settings**
- Currency preference (USD, EUR, BDT, INR, etc.)
- Language selection (English, Bengali, Hindi)
- Theme preference (Light/Dark mode)
- Date format settings
- Display preferences
- Notification settings
- Budget start day preference

### **✅ App Data**
- Onboarding completion status
- User preferences cache
- Temporary data
- Search history
- Filter preferences

---

## 🔐 What is NOT Deleted

The following items are necessary for the app to function properly and are **NOT deleted** when you reset your data:

- **App installation files** - The app itself remains installed
- **Pre-loaded categories** - Default category list (Food, Transport, etc.)
- **System app data** - Required for app operation
- **App cache** - Will regenerate automatically
- **Contact permissions** - App permission settings remain

---

## 📋 Data Retention Policy

### **After You Delete Data:**

✅ **No data is retained** - All user financial data is permanently removed

✅ **No cloud backups** - We don't store backups in the cloud (app is offline-first)

✅ **No server copies** - No data is sent to external servers

✅ **No recovery possible** - Deletion is permanent and irreversible

✅ **Immediate deletion** - Data is deleted instantly (no 90-day retention period)

✅ **Local device only** - All data stays on your device; we have no copy

### **Important:**
Mango Finance is an **offline-first application**. All your financial data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, store, or maintain any copies of your data on our servers or in the cloud. When you delete your data, it is permanently removed from your device.

---

## 🛡️ Data Privacy Before Deletion

Before you delete your data, here's how we protect it:

### **Storage Security:**
- All data stored locally in SQLite database on your device
- Located in your app's private directory (not accessible to other apps)
- Protected by your device's security measures
- No cloud storage or server backup

### **Transmission Security:**
- **No data transmission** - Your data never leaves your device
- **No network requests** - App works completely offline
- **No third-party sharing** - We never share your data
- **No analytics tracking** - We don't track your usage

### **Device Security:**
- Use your device's lock screen password
- Enable biometric security if available
- Keep your device updated with latest security patches
- Don't root/jailbreak your device

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### **Q: Will deleting data affect the app installation?**
A: No. The app will remain installed and fully functional. You can continue using it after deletion.

### **Q: Can I delete only some data (like transactions but keep budgets)?**
A: No. The "Reset All Data" feature deletes everything at once. There is no option to delete selectively.

### **Q: Is there a way to recover deleted data?**
A: No. Deletion is permanent. Once deleted, data cannot be recovered. We recommend backing up important financial data before deleting.

### **Q: Does Mango Finance store my data on cloud servers?**
A: No. Mango Finance is completely offline. All data stays on your device only.

### **Q: Will deleting data from Mango Finance affect my bank account or other services?**
A: No. Deleting data from Mango Finance only removes the records from the app. It does not affect your actual bank accounts, credit cards, or any external financial services.

### **Q: How long does data deletion take?**
A: Data is deleted immediately. The process takes less than a second.

### **Q: Do you need my permission to delete data?**
A: No. You have full control. You can delete your data anytime without requesting permission from us.

### **Q: Is there a waiting period (like 30 or 90 days)?**
A: No. Data is deleted immediately and permanently. There is no waiting period.

### **Q: Can I undo the deletion?**
A: No. Once deleted, data cannot be recovered. Be certain before selecting "Reset All Data."

### **Q: What if I want to back up my data before deleting?**
A: You can export your data to CSV or PDF before deleting:
1. Go to Settings → Data
2. Select "Export Data"
3. Choose CSV or PDF format
4. Share or save the file
5. Then you can safely delete the app data

## 📋 Additional Information

### **For EU Users (GDPR Compliance):**
Under GDPR, you have the right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"). Mango Finance fully supports this right through the in-app deletion feature. Your data is yours alone, and you can request deletion at any time.

### **For California Users (CCPA Compliance):**
Under CCPA, you have the right to know what personal information is collected and to request deletion. Mango Finance is transparent about data collection (financial transactions and preferences) and provides immediate deletion on request.

### **For All Users:**
We are committed to protecting your privacy. Mango Finance:
- Collects only necessary data for app function
- Does not sell or share your data
- Does not track your activities
- Does not display advertisements
- Provides full data deletion capability
